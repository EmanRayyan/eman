About Us

We are all part of a society where voting is the main method for making decisions and reaching a consensus. Today, most voting is still done offline. Polys’s mission is to change the way people vote.

We want to facilitate help governments, businesses, educational institutions, and communities dispense with paperwork and facilitate efficient online elections. That’s why we’ve created a secure, transparent and credible voting system based on blockchain technology.
